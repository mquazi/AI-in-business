AI in Business: Intelligent Transformation and Management

AI is at the forefront of business transformation, reshaping how organizations manage operations, enhance customer engagement, and drive innovation. This stream, AI in Business: Intelligent Transformation and Management, explores the strategic deployment of AI across various business domains to create competitive advantage and foster sustainable growth. Attendees will gain insights from industry experts and thought leaders on the latest applications, trends, and challenges in leveraging AI for intelligent management and transformation.
